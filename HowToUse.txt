How to use Battery Data Processing Software (Dedicated to ARBIN Cycler) :

1. Start by selecting an .xls (or .xlsx) file of your choice by going to (File -> Choose File...). Once you have 

selected the file, the file will be shown in the box labeled "Chosen File:"

2. Next, insert the mass of your choice in grams. Ex. 20

3. Then, insert three cycles of your choice. Ex. 1 3 7

4. After that, insert how many channel sheets and statistic sheets there are.

5. Press the "Apply" button and wait until it says "Saved!".

6. Finally select one graph in "Choose Graph" and press "Create Graphs".
