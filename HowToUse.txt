How to use (program name) :

1. Start by selecting an .xls file of your choice by going to (File -> Choose File...). Once you have selected the file,

the file will be shown in the box labeled "Chosen File:"

2. Next, insert the mass of your choice in grams. Ex. 20

3. Then, insert three cycles of your choice. Ex. 1 3 6

4. Once you have selected three cycles of your choice, press the "Apply" button and wait until it says "Saved!".

5. Finally select three graphs from the dropdown items labeled, "Bottom Pane", "Top Left Pane" , and "Top Right Pane" and press "Create Graphs".
