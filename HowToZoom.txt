How to Zoom

1. Hold down your left mouse button and drag a box to where you want to zoom in.

2. Double left click to zoom back to default. 


NOTICE: Volatge vs Capacity may be difficult to zoom. You must have some of the Charge Capacity in the zoombox in order to zoom into Dischare Capacity 

and vice versa.
